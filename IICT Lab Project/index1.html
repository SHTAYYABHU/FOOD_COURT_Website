About

Our story began in early 2000s when we opened our first restaurant in countryside with dishes on menu were just four soup and bread slowly people started coming to our restaurant and we started adding more and more dishes to our deals in late 2005 we open three new outlet in city with all three system dine in takeaway and delivery. And in 2017 we received award for best hygienic food by award for food by Pakistan Food Authority (PFA) by 2019 had almost 9 outlets with around 1200 worker working 24/7. We later decided to launch our website for easy way for our customer to place order for their food. And soon we will be launching our application in near future.
